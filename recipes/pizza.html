Pizza

What is a pizza

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin, consisting of a flat round base of dough baked with a topping of tomatoes and cheese, typically with added meat, fish, or vegetables.

Ingredients of pizza

  • pizza dough
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese

How to make a pizza

  1. Make a pizza dough
  2. Put tomato sauce on the dough
  3. Put cheese on the dough
  4. Bake it in owen for 20 mins
