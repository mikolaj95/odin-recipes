Ham Sandwich

What is a Ham Sandwich

Ham Sandwich is a dish consisting of a flat round cake of minced beef, or sometimes another savoury ingredient, that is fried or grilled and served in a split bun or roll with various condiments and toppings.

Ingredients of Ham Sandwich

  • bread
  • butter
  • mayo
  • ham

How to make a Ham Sandwich

  1. On bread put butter and mayo
  2. Put ham
  3. Fold the sandwich
