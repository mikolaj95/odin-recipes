Burger

What is a burger

Burger is a dish consisting of a flat round cake of minced beef, or sometimes another savoury ingredient, that is fried or grilled and served in a split bun or roll with various condiments and toppings.

Ingredients of burger

  • beef
  • buns
  • mayonnaise
  • onion
  • tomato
  • lettuce
  • bacon

How to make a burger

  1. Make beef
  2. Heat up buns
  3. Put mayo inside the bun
  4. Put beef on bun
  5. Put lettuce on beef
  6. Put tomato on lettuce
  7. Put onion on tomato
  8. Put bacon on onion
